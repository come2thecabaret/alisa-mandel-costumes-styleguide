Introduction

The purpose of this document is to outline a style guide for the Alisa Mandel Costume Design website. Similarly to how you provide fabric samples to clients, this page is designed to outline the colors, typography, and animation styles for your Costume Design site.

NOTE: This document does not reflect the layout of the website deliverable, but rather specifies the overall 'look and feel' of the site. This document will allow the customer, designer, and developer to better collaborate and ensure we are on the same page.

Colors
Base Palette

A neutral site color palette was selected in order to not detract from the bright and vibrant colors of the costumes. Representing the design of the costumes in the best light possible is the most important goal of the site.

White (#ffffff)
This will be used for the main background of the site.
Beige (#e5ded4)
This will be used to highlight gallery items (see page layout below).
Black (#333333)
This will be used as the main font color for paragraphs on the white or beige background.
